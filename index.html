our tasks

Designing

Our very creative design team can collaborate with you in live-sessions to design your websites, apps and more and improve upon it with industry-standard UI/UX practices.

Branding

If you starting a new venture, having a brand is very important for you to stand out among the sea of competitors in the modern web. We can help you with creating a positive brand image based on your values, goals and the target demographic.

Content Writing

Have an existing website or blog? Do you feel like the content in it isn't up to the mark? We can help you with adding content to your existing website or a prototype to replace all your "Lorem Ipsum"s.

Building

Have a website design that you want to build? Do you like the website we have designed for you? Then we can turn that into a working website. Be it a static website to showcase your buisness, a personal blog where you are constantly adding stories, images and more or a complete web application, we have got you covered.

Hosting

We provide a complete solution when it comes to your online presence. We provide servers to host your websites, manage your domain, provide SSL for your website, etc and make sure the servers are as secure as possible.

Cloud Solutions

Do you or you company needed private cloud storage, internal management systems, mail services, full-on office suite of applications in the cloud, password management and more. We can provide you all these on a personal server without needing to rely on any enterprises, tailored and priced according to your needs.
